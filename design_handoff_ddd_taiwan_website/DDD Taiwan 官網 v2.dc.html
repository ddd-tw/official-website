Domain-Driven Design · Taiwan

從問題領域出發，
催生真正有價值的解決方案。 From the problem domain,
toward solutions that matter.

DDD Taiwan 致力於在台灣推廣領域驅動設計。無論你是工程師、產品經理、設計師、分析師，或任何想把問題想清楚的人，都歡迎加入 — 透過 Meetup、讀書會與年度大會，我們一起探索與確認業務問題、凝聚共識，再把共識帶進可實踐的解決方案。 DDD Taiwan promotes Domain-Driven Design in Taiwan. Whether you're an engineer, product manager, designer, analyst — or anyone who wants to understand problems deeply — you're welcome here. Through meetups, book clubs and our annual conference, we explore and validate business problems together, build shared understanding, and carry that consensus into working solutions.

治理與政策 — 規範層Governance & policy

透明的社群運作How we run things

確保社群運作透明，保障成員權益與互動品質。Transparent operations that protect members' rights and the quality of every interaction.

隱私權政策Privacy Policy

蒐集與使用。Collection & use. 我們僅於活動報名與通知時蒐集必要的個人資料（姓名、Email），用於活動聯繫、錄影連結寄送與社群公告，不會轉售或提供給第三方行銷使用。We collect only what event registration requires (name, email), used for event contact, recording links and community announcements — never sold or shared for third-party marketing.

保護與法遵。Protection & compliance. 個人資料之蒐集、處理及利用符合個人資料保護法與 GDPR 精神；報名資料由 KKTIX 平台代為處理。Handling follows Taiwan's Personal Data Protection Act and the spirit of GDPR; registration data is processed by KKTIX.

你的權利。Your rights. 你可隨時來信 dddtw2018@gmail.com 要求查詢、更正或刪除你的個人資料。Email dddtw2018@gmail.com anytime to access, correct or delete your data.

社群行為準則Code of Conduct

期望行為。Expected behavior. 尊重每位參與者的背景與觀點，聚焦於想法的討論而非個人。歡迎提問 — 沒有「太基本」的問題。Respect every participant's background and views; debate ideas, not people. All questions are welcome — none is "too basic."

不容忍行為。Not tolerated. 任何形式的騷擾、歧視與人身攻擊。此規範同樣適用於講者 — 講者不得於分享中攻擊他人或其他技術社群。Harassment, discrimination and personal attacks of any kind. This applies to speakers too — talks must not attack individuals or other communities.

違規處理。Enforcement. 由志工團隊視情節提醒、勸離或停止其參與資格；重大違規將公告處理結果。Volunteers will warn, remove or bar participants as severity warrants; major incidents are reported back to the community.

申訴管道。Reporting. 活動現場可直接向志工反映，或來信 dddtw2018@gmail.com — 所有申訴均予保密。Flag issues to any volunteer on site, or email dddtw2018@gmail.com — all reports are confidential.
